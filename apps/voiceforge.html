Features

Everything You Need

Professional-grade audiobook production without a recording studio, voice actors, or editing software.

🎭

Emotional Narration

Tag your text with emotion markers like <whisper>, <excited>, <pause>. Our AI interprets context automatically for natural dramatic delivery.

👥

Multi-Character Voices

Assign different AI voices to each character in your book. VoiceForge auto-detects dialogue speakers and switches seamlessly between voices.

📖

EPUB & PDF Import

Drag and drop your ebook files directly. We parse chapters, metadata, and table of contents automatically — no formatting required.

🎵

Background Music & SFX

Layer ambient soundscapes — forest rain, tavern ambience, space hum — that adapt dynamically to your story's mood and genre.

⚡

Long-Form Stability

Our AI maintains consistent voice quality and character across entire novels. No drift, no glitching — flawless narration for 10+ hour audiobooks.

📡

Real-Time Streaming

Start listening to your audiobook as it generates — no waiting for full export. Stream chapter by chapter the moment processing begins.
