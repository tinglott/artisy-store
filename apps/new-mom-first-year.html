📋 Privacy & Data

Your privacy matters: This app stores your gift data locally on your device. No personal information is sent to external servers unless you choose to:

  • Upload photos (optional, uses Cloudinary for image optimization)
  • Import social media (Facebook/Instagram—optional)
  • Generate AI stories (uses Grok API—optional)

You control everything: All gift data can be downloaded, shared, or deleted at any time.
